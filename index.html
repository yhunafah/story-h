lazy john

There was a boy named John, and he was the laziest boy in the entire town. One day, John saw their apple tree full of shiny red apples. He wanted to eat some but was too lazy to climb the tree. Instead, he lay on the grass and waited for the apples to fall down on their own. But, of course, the apples didn’t fall, and soon John was very, very hungry because he hadn’t worked for his food. Moral of the story: Laziness won’t get you anywhere. You have to work hard for what you wanted
